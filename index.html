Who We Are

We are the Planned Parenthood Generation Action chapter at the University of Arizona. Our club works to promote reproductive health, rights, and education on campus and in the Tucson community.

What We Do

  • Host educational events and discussions
  • Advocate for reproductive health access
  • Partner with campus and community groups
  • Support voter engagement and policy awareness
