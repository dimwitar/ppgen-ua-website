What We Believe In

PPGen is grounded in a reproductive justice framework. Reproductive justice is the right to have a child if and when you want, the right to not have a child, and the right to parent and build families in safe and healthy communities.

Our work includes advocacy for comprehensive sex education, abortion access, STI testing, maternal health, domestic violence prevention, privacy rights, and the rights of marginalized communities.

What We Do

  • Mobilize students for reproductive justice advocacy
  • Educate the campus community on sexual and reproductive health
  • Partner with campus and community organizations
  • Support policy, voter, and issue based campaigns
